High Stakes: The Comic

A Kickstarter Project to Fight Gambling Addiction

Project Description

[cite_start]

What it is: An interactive comic designed to prevent gambling addiction[cite: 14, 22].

[cite_start]

Problem: Over 60 million people suffer from gambling addiction globally, which can lead to tragic loss of life[cite: 23, 24].

[cite_start]

Uniqueness: Our comic uses visual storytelling to show the psychological trap of casinos, making the danger visible and understandable for all ages[cite: 16, 25].
